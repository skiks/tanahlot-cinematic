Tanah Lot

Daya Tarik Wisata Tanah Lot — official management of a 16th-century Hindu sea temple founded by Dang Hyang Nirartha, now one of Bali's seven sacred sea temples.

"Nestled amidst the majestic waves of the Indian Ocean, Tanah Lot Temple stands as an iconic emblem of Bali's spiritual and natural allure. Perched atop a rugged offshore rock formation, this ancient Hindu sanctuary captivates visitors with its dramatic silhouette against the sunset-streaked horizon."
